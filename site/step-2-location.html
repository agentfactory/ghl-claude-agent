Step 2: Find Your Location ID

Your Location ID tells Claude which GHL account (or sub-account) to connect to. There are two easy ways to find it.

This takes about 1 minute.

Method 1: Settings Page (Recommended)

  1. Log in to your GoHighLevel account
  2. Click Settings in the left sidebar
  3. Click Company (or Business Info depending on your account type)
  4. Look for the Location ID field on that page

What you should see: A string of letters and numbers, something like abc123DEF456ghi789.

Method 2: URL Bar

If you cannot find it in settings, check your browser URL bar.

  1. Log in to GHL and go to any page in your account
  2. Look at the URL in your browser address bar
  3. Find the section that says /location/
  4. The string right after /location/ is your Location ID

Example URL:

https://app.gohighlevel.com/location/abc123DEF456ghi789/dashboard

In this example, your Location ID is abc123DEF456ghi789.

What you should see: A string of characters between /location/ and the next / in your URL.

Save Your Location ID

Copy your Location ID and save it alongside your PIT token from Step 1. You will need both in the next step.

You should now have two pieces of information saved:

Which Location to Use

If you manage multiple sub-accounts in GHL, use the Location ID for the specific account you want Claude to access.

You can always set up additional connections later for other locations. Start with one.
