## COMPLIANCE NOTES

- All ad copy avoids the words "AI" and "automation" per brand guidelines
- No em dashes used anywhere in this document
- No sentences exceed 25 words in public-facing copy
- No hype language (no "revolutionary," "game-changing," "unlock," etc.)
- No false scarcity (no "only X spots left," "doors closing," etc.)
- All CTAs point to verified Skool URLs (free and paid)
- Tone throughout: calm, direct, competent
