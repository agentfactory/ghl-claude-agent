# What Claude Can Do Inside GoHighLevel

Once connected, Claude has access to 269 tools across 19 categories in your GHL account. That covers nearly everything you can do in the GHL dashboard.

Here is what that looks like in practice.

## The 4 Most Common Use Cases

Most GHL users start with these four workflows. Each one replaces a task that normally takes 5 to 15 minutes of manual clicking.

### 1. Lead Qualification

**Without Claude:** Open the contact, read their info, check their activity, decide if they are hot/warm/cold, add a tag, write a note.

**With Claude:** "Look at the new lead John Smith. Based on his form submission and any conversation history, tag him as Hot, Warm, or Cold and add a summary note."

Claude does all of that in one request.

### 2. Follow-Up Messages

**Without Claude:** Open the contact, read past conversations, think about what to say, type a personalized message, send it.

**With Claude:** "Write a follow-up message to Sarah Johnson based on our last conversation. Match my usual tone and reference what we discussed."

Claude reads the history and drafts the message for you.

### 3. Pipeline Management

**Without Claude:** Open the pipeline view, scroll through stages, identify stalled deals, move them one by one, make notes on each.

**With Claude:** "Show me every deal in the Proposal Sent stage that has not been touched in 7 days. Move them to Needs Attention and add a note about the delay."

Claude handles the entire cleanup in seconds.

### 4. Appointment Booking

**Without Claude:** Check your calendar, find open slots, send available times to the lead, wait for a reply, book it manually.

**With Claude:** "Check my calendar for open slots this week and book a 30-minute call with Mike Davis on the first available afternoon slot."

Claude checks availability and books it directly.

## What Claude Cannot Do (Yet)

A few things to know about current limitations:

- **No undo button.** If Claude moves a contact or changes a field, there is no built-in rollback. Always test with non-critical data first.
- **Rate limits apply.** GHL limits how many requests can happen per minute. Claude handles this gracefully, but bulk operations on hundreds of contacts may take time.
- **No granular permissions yet.** The Private Integration Token gives access to all enabled scopes. You cannot restrict Claude to read-only on specific categories while allowing writes on others.
- **OAuth is not yet supported.** You connect using a Private Integration Token, not OAuth.

These limitations are expected to improve as GHL continues building out their MCP support.
