Use Case: Qualify a New Lead

This prompt tells Claude to pull a new contact from your GHL account, assess their information, tag them based on quality, and add a summary note.

Time saved: 5 to 10 minutes per lead, compared to doing it manually.

What This Does

  1. Claude looks up the contact in your GHL account
  2. Reviews their form submission data, tags, and any conversation history
  3. Decides if the lead is Hot, Warm, or Cold based on your criteria
  4. Adds the appropriate tag to the contact
  5. Writes a summary note on the contact record

The Prompt

Copy this prompt and paste it into Claude. Replace the placeholder values in brackets with your own details.

Look up the contact [CONTACT NAME] in my GoHighLevel account.

Review their profile, form submissions, and any conversation history.

Based on the following criteria, tag them as one of: Hot-Lead, Warm-Lead, or Cold-Lead.

Hot-Lead criteria:
- They mentioned a specific budget or timeline
- They responded to a message within 24 hours
- They match our ideal customer profile: [DESCRIBE YOUR IDEAL CUSTOMER]

Warm-Lead criteria:
- They filled out a form but have not responded to follow-up
- They show interest but no urgency

Cold-Lead criteria:
- No response after initial contact
- Incomplete information
- Does not match our ideal customer profile

After tagging, add a note to the contact that summarizes:
- What information you found
- Why you chose that tag
- Suggested next step

Example Output

After running this prompt, Claude might respond with something like:

I found Sarah Johnson in your contacts. She submitted a form on March 15 requesting pricing for your agency plan. In her submission, she mentioned a budget of $2,000/month and wants to start within 2 weeks.

She replied to your initial outreach within 3 hours.

I have tagged her as Hot-Lead and added this note to her contact record:

"Lead assessment: Hot. Budget confirmed ($2K/mo), timeline confirmed (2 weeks), fast response time (3 hours). Suggested next step: Schedule a discovery call this week."

Customizing the Prompt

Change the tag names. Replace Hot-Lead, Warm-Lead, and Cold-Lead with whatever tags you already use in your GHL account.

Adjust the criteria. Swap in your own qualification standards. The more specific you are, the better Claude performs.

Add more categories. You can add a fourth tier like VIP-Lead or Not-Qualified if your sales process uses them.

Running This on Multiple Leads

You can also ask Claude to qualify several contacts at once:

Look at all contacts tagged "new-lead" in my GoHighLevel account.
For each one, review their profile and conversation history.
Tag each as Hot-Lead, Warm-Lead, or Cold-Lead using the criteria above.
Add a summary note to each contact explaining the rating.
Give me a summary table when you are done.
Bulk operations may take longer depending on how many contacts match. GHL rate limits apply. Start with a small batch (10 or fewer) to test.
